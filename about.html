About Our School

Established in 2006, Rangtse Primary School serves as a pillar of education for the children of Gakiling Gewog in Haa, Bhutan. We are committed to nurturing young minds and fostering academic excellence in a safe, inclusive environment.

Quick Facts

Established: 2006
Classes: PP to VI
Students: 118
Teaching Staff: 8
Non-Teaching Staff: 8

Our Vision

To empower young minds with knowledge, values, and skills to build a brighter future for themselves and their communities.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that emphasizes academic excellence, character development, and cultural preservation in a nurturing environment.
